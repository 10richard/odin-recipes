Rice and Chicken

Description

Do you want an easy-to-make and healthy meal? Look no further, for we present to you the undefeated combo. Rice and chicken! This recipe is so simple that you will only need one pot and one pan!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Rice
  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 cups Spinach
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Boil water in the pot
  2. Add rice to the pot (leave for 9-12 minutes)
  3. Place pan under medium-high heat and drizzle some oil onto the pan
  4. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper and slice into medium-sized cubes
  5. Place chicken into the pan (4-6 minutes)
  6. Drain rice and season with salt and pepper
  7. Take chicken out of the pan and into the pot
  8. Finally mix the chicken, rice and spinach
