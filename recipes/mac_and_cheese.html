Mac & Cheese

Description

Tired of eating mac and cheese out of a box? Perhaps you're too lazy to drive all the way down to the store. Whatever the case, this recipe was designed to blow you away! It is simple, practical and amazingly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Dried Macaroni
  • 1 cup Whole Milk
  • 1 pound Cheese (whichever is your favorite)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add macaroni into pot (leave for 9-12 minutes)
  3. Drain the pot with macaroni
  4. Place the pot back onto the stove and add a tablespoon of butter
  5. Add your desired cheese and mix well
  6. Add the whole milk while mixing (don't stop mixing)
  7. Finally, season with salt and pepper
