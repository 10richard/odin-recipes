Garlic Shrimp

Description

By far the easiest snack to make on the list.

Ingredients

  • Peeled Shrimp
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Lime

Directions

  1. Drizzle oil into medium-sized pan (set heat to medium-high)
  2. Place shrimp into pan (cook for 6-8 minutes)
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Add minced garlic into pan
  5. Place into plate once shrimp is finished cooking
  6. Squeeze lime over the shrimp
